## Section 2. Salience Audit Against the Artifact

One line each, aimed at the finished work.

1. Hostile reader: what did I check first that a hostile reviewer would check first?
2. Quiet giant: the least discussed element with the largest consequence, examined or not?
3. Unnamed party: any affected party the artifact never names?
4. Naked assertion: material claims riding on register rather than record?
5. Missing heading: what does the structure omit that a specialist would expect?
6. Inherited agenda: what did the artifact make me attend to, and what did it keep me from considering?
7. Absent evidence: is everything attached that must exist if the work is right?
